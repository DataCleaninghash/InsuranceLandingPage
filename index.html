Frequently Asked Questions

What insurance types do you support? +
Health, auto, home/renters, and travel. We work with major U.S. insurers and TPAs.
Do I still need to call the insurer? +
No. With your consent, Voceo handles calls, forms, and follow-ups. You get updates in your dashboard.
Is this legal advice? +
No. Voceo is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. We act as your authorized consumer advocate to move your claim forward.
What documents should I prepare? +
Policy/ID, claim number, denial letter/EOB, bills/receipts, photos/estimates, and a short description of what happened.
Can you file an appeal or complaint? +
Yes. We prepare/submitted appeals and, when appropriate, file consumer complaints with official channels. We also coordinate with providers or employer benefits admins for health plans.
What if I already tried and got nowhere? +
We escalate to manager queues and provide corrected documentation/codes where needed. Many cases move after structured follow-up.
Are you affiliated with insurers? +
No. We are independent and use official insurer and consumer protection channels.
